Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Afams Ltd uses cookies at afams.co.ke to ensure the Platform functions correctly, to understand usage patterns, and to improve services.

  • Security tokens and fraud prevention

2.2 Analytical and Performance Cookies

Used to collect anonymised statistical data to improve Platform performance. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytical cookies.

  • Page visit counts and navigation paths

2.3 Functional Cookies

Enable enhanced functionality and personalisation, such as remembering user preferences.

4.1 Platform Cookie Consent Tool

Upon your first visit to the Platform, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You may accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customise your preferences by category. Your preferences will be stored in a consent cookie for future visits.
