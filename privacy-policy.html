6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

Upon expiry of the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised, unless retention is required by law or for the establishment, exercise, or defence of legal claims by Afams Ltd.

Afams Ltd does not warrant absolute security of data and shall not be liable for any unauthorised access, loss, theft, or disclosure of personal data resulting from circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including third-party cyberattacks, zero-day vulnerabilities, or Force Majeure Events.

Subject to the Data Protection Act, 2019 (Kenya), you have the following rights:
