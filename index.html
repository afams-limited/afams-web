Our story
Built in Nairobi.
Farming into the future.

Afams Ltd was founded in 2021 by Moses W. Mwombe in Nairobi, Kenya — with a clear and urgent mission: to make urban food production accessible to every household, school, restaurant, and office, regardless of land, water, or expertise.

We watched cities grow faster than their food systems. We watched urban Kenyans — and urban people everywhere — disconnected from the food they eat. We decided to build the bridge. Not with expensive technology. Not with complexity. With a bag.

The FarmBag is that bridge. It folds flat. It ships anywhere. It grows anything. It waters itself. And now — with NutriPort™, the Grow Cube™, and the AquaFarmBag — it is growing into something that will one day feed people on Mars.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is a pre-order and when do I get my bag? +
A pre-order means you pay now and we prepare your FarmBag immediately. We fulfill every order within 3 business days of payment. Nairobi delivery is included. You receive order confirmation and tracking by email and WhatsApp as soon as your bag is dispatched.
Can I use the FarmBag indoors? +
Yes — all FarmBag versions have a fully sealed canvas exterior with a polythene inner liner. No soil, water, or mess escapes. The FarmBag Vertical and Hydro Pro are specifically designed for indoor use on hardwood, tile, or carpet floors. The Classic also includes an overflow port that prevents waterlogging.
What can I grow in the FarmBag? +
The Classic and Vertical support any crop: sukuma wiki, tomatoes, kale, spinach, lettuce, capsicum, herbs, chillies, beans, and more. The Hydro Pro is optimised for fast-turnaround crops: lettuce, basil, dhania, mint, spinach, and microgreens. The AquaFarmBag grows any crop the Hydro Pro does, with the addition of tilapia, catfish, or ornamental fish.
How do NutriPort™ cartridges work? +
NutriPort cartridges screw into threaded ports on the FarmBag Hydro Pro. Each cartridge contains a concentrated nutrient solution and a porous ceramic diffuser stone at the nozzle. The solution diffuses passively through the stone into the reservoir — creating visible micro-bubbles that oxygenate the root zone. No pump, no timer, no electricity required. Replace each cartridge every 3–6 weeks.
Do you deliver outside Nairobi? +
Yes. Kenya-wide delivery via courier partners: 3–5 business days to Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and other major towns. UK and UAE delivery is quoted per order — contact us at afamskenya@gmail.com for international shipping rates. We are building our international fulfilment infrastructure now.
Can I return my FarmBag if I’m not happy? +
Yes. We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee on all FarmBag hardware products. If your bag has a manufacturing defect or you are not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of delivery and we will arrange a replacement or full refund. Pre-order payments are refundable if you change your mind before your bag is dispatched.
